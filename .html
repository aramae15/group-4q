Accessibility Issues

PWD'S

PHYSICAL

A health problem related to the body encompassing diseases, injuries, impairments, and chronic pain, often influenced by physical inactivity, poor nutrition, and stress.

COMMUNICATION

PWDs face communication issues like no captions for the deaf, no alt text for the blind, lack of text options for speech-impaired, and complex language for cognitive disabilities.

DIGITAL

The digital divide, which is the unequal access to and use of technology, and a lack of digital accessibility in websites and services designed without PWD needs in mind.

ECONOMIC

Higher poverty rates, lack of access to stable employment, prevalence of informal jobs and underemployment, leading to a cycle of poverty.

ATTITUDINAL

Negative perceptions, stereotypes, stigma, prejudice, and discrimination from others that hinder their social integration and well-being.
